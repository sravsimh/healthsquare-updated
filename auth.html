Why Prior Authorization is Essential

Prior authorization is a critical step in the healthcare delivery process. It involves obtaining approval from insurance companies before a healthcare provider can deliver certain treatments, medications, or services. This process helps manage costs and ensure that patients receive appropriate care. However, it can be time-consuming and complex, diverting valuable resources from patient care.
