Comprehensive Services

Our payment posting services cover all aspects of the process, including:

  1. Electronic and Manual Posting: Whether payments come through electronic remittance advice (ERA) or paper explanations of benefits (EOBs), we handle them with equal proficiency.
  2. Denial and Rejection Management: We identify and resolve payment denials and rejections swiftly, minimizing their impact on your revenue.
  3. Payment Reconciliation: We ensure that posted payments are accurately reconciled with bank deposits, providing clear and transparent financial records.
  4. Adjustments and Write-offs: Our team expertly manages adjustments and write-offs, ensuring they are applied correctly and consistently.
